Interactive Random Quote Generator

Welcome to my Interactive Random Quote Generator! This web application allows you to explore, create, and manage collections of inspirational, humorous, and wise quotes. Easily add, update, or delete quotes and lists, and save your favorites for later enjoyment. Perfect for finding daily inspiration or sharing meaningful quotes with others.

Features at a Glance:

  • Create Multiple Quote Lists: Organize quotes into customizable lists.
  • Edit Quotes: Add, update, or remove quotes easily.
  • Interactive Display: View and share quotes from any list on the webpage.
  • Save and Load: Keep your quotes safe with local storage capabilities.

Dive into a world of quotes tailored just for you!
